About medical laboratory science

The medical laboratory science Department emphasizes innovation in robotics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing technologies. Established in 1960, we have a proud history of academic and research excellence.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be a leader in medical laboratory science research and education.

Our mission is to educate students and conduct research that advances technology and benefits society.
