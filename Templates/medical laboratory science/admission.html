Admissions

Entry Requirements

Applicants should have completed secondary education with strong results in physics, mathematics, and chemistry.

  • Bachelor's degree programme: Relevant high school diploma with science courses.
  • Master's degree programme: Bachelor's degree in Electrical or related engineering fields.

Application Process

  1. Submit your application online via the university portal.
  2. Provide academic transcripts and reference letters.
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Prepare for interviews if applicable.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Scholarships based on merit and financial need are available to qualified students. Contact the admissions office to learn more.
