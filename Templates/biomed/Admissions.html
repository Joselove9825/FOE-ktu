Biomedical Engineering Admissions

Join us in shaping the future of healthcare technology!

Welcome to the Biomedical Engineering Department

Our Biomedical Engineering program combines engineering principles with medical sciences to advance healthcare. We are dedicated to preparing the next generation of innovators and leaders in the field.

Admission Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering or a related field
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • GRE scores (optional for certain applicants)
  • Letters of recommendation (2 required)
  • Personal statement outlining your interest in biomedical engineering

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit your official transcripts.
  3. Upload your personal statement and letters of recommendation.
  4. Pay the application fee.
  5. Check your application status online.
